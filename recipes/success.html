Servings

As many as you're willing to make

Prep time

Ongoing

Cook time

Variable - requires patience

Serving Suggestion

Best enjoyed with humility, gratitude, and willingness to go back for seconds (or start over).

Ingredients

  • 1 solid foundation of clear goals
  • 2 cups of consistent effort
  • 1/2 cup of self-awareness
  • 3 tablespoons of adaptability
  • 1 scoop of passion
  • 1/4 cup of failure (yes, it's essential)
  • A sprinkle of luck
  • A handful of supportive people
  • Optional: mentorship, coffee-fueled ambition

Steps

  1. Start with clear goals and lay them out evenly across your workspace.
  2. Slowly add consistent effort, stirring daily—even when motivation runs low.
  3. Fold in self-awareness and adaptability. These will help your mixture adjust as conditions change.
  4. Add passion for flavor and longevity. Don’t let it burn—keep it on a low, steady heat.
  5. Mix in failure — don’t skip this! It adds depth and texture.
  6. Top with a sprinkle of luck and a good dose of community or mentorship.
  7. Let rise. Success takes time. Don’t open the oven too early.
